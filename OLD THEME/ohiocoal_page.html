Below are selected screen captures from the OCDO outreach program. Developer of the UI/UX for a large body of technical work to support the clients initiatives to disseminate information through an outreach program.

About the project: The goal of the project is to develop process understanding and evaluate technical and economic feasibility of CO2 utilization for enhanced oil recovery (EOR) and geologic storage in Ohio. The focus is on depleted oil fields in the Clinton Sandstone (Eastern Ohio) and the Knox Dolomite Group (North-Central Ohio).
