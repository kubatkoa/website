Below are selected screen captures from the EMA VAT Storyboard. Project contributions include Project Manager, Principal Investigator, and the visionary & creator behind the user-friendly website interface, which overlays complex mathematics, allowing for rapid analysis and improved understanding of EMA threats.

About the project: The Grocery Manufacturers Association (GMA) represents the world’s largest branded food, beverage and consumer products companies. The team is currently interacting with GMA leadership to develop an industry-wide software solution to assess vulnerability of various foods to economically motivated adulteration.
