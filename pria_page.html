PRIA

Below are selected images from the PRIA program. Project contributions include Co-Project Manager, Principal Investigator and the designer behind the UI/UX, as well as one of the named inventors of the computational mathematics underlying the mathematical modeling in the PRIA software.

About the project: PRIA software is a state-of-the-art system developed specifically to address the food-safety concerns of poultry and other food producers. Rather than relying on inefficient and limited spreadsheet-based methods, PRIA arms the producers team with intuitive software-based tools to evaluate the company’s risk at each phase of processing.
